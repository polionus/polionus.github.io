About

I am Master of Computing Science student at the University of Alberta. My research interests lie broadly in machine learning, specifically in (deep) reinforcement learning, representation learning, continual learning, and real‑world applications of all the above.

Previously, I studied Computer Science at Islamic Azad University, and worked in the Machine Learning startup Chashmyar, and the E-learning platform Shastad.ir, where I created educational content and also was an AI engineer for their recommendation system.

I completed my B.Sc. and M.Sc. at UFMG, Brazil, and my Ph.D. at the University of Alberta, where I popularized the idea of temporally‑extended exploration through options, introducing the concept of eigenoptions.

Research Interests

  • Deep Reinforcement Learning
  • Representation & Feature Learning
  • Continual & Lifelong Learning
  • AI for Real‑World Control Problems

Selected Highlights
